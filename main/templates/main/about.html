Data access & data management

Google Fit data: The Google Fit API gives this project ongoing access to data, which we will collect and store for you in Open Humans in an ongoing manner. Depending on how you use the app, this may include: steps, active minutes, estimated calories expended, and distance traveled.

Data files created by the app are stored in your Open Humans account and are always visible to you on the project activity page.

Depending on how you use the app, Google Fit's API also gives us access to data we will not copy into your Open Humans account, including: height, weight, nutrition, and heart rate. Access to this data is unavoidable due to how the API operates, but we won't store it.

Other data: This project stores tokens for Open Humans and Google Fit authorizations, but has no further access to your Open Humans account data beyond the data files it stores.

Removing this connection: You can visit the project activity page in Open Humans to remove this connection within Open Humans and, optionally, delete associated data files.
